Hi, I am Sajad Ali. I am a Graduate Researcher in Electrical Machine Design & Optimization at Sukkur IBA University. My work specializes in synchronous machines, Finite Element Analysis (FEA), and ML-based surrogate modeling.

## Research Interests
- **Synchronous Machines:** Design and optimization of modern synchronous machines.
- **Finite Element Analysis (FEA):** Detailed electromagnetic and thermal analysis using advanced FEA software.
- **Machine Learning Surrogate Models:** Leveraging machine learning techniques for rapid and accurate machine design optimization.
